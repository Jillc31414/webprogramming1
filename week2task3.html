Exploring Guam

Top Attractions

  1. Two Lovers Point

    2. This beautiful landmark overlooks the ocean and is the best view you can get! They often have little shops open and have a gift shop. Tip: Don't forget to buy a heart to write your name on and attach it to the fence!

  2. UnderWater World

    3. This aquarium is located inside the mall! You get free IHop pancakes next door when you purchase a ticket to the aquarium. It is small but includes a tunnel to walkthrough where the fish and sharks swim over and around you!

  3. Gab Gab Beach

    4. This beautiful beach has picnic tables for shade, plenty of trees, and clear water! Snorkeling is great hear and it's shallow so you can see plenty of fish!

Things To Do

  1. Stroll the Beaches

    2. There are many beaches to explore including Tanguisson Beach and Shark's Cove Beach.

  2. Snorkle

    3. Snorkle at the many beaches around the island including Gab Gab Beach and Gun Beach. There are also plenty of rental places for any equipment needed!

  3. Enjoy the food!

    4. Food in Guam is influenced by the Chamorro culture and other cuisines such as Spanish, Filipino, and Asian. Tip: My favorite spots on the island are Crust, where they serve an authentic italian wood fire pizza, and Slowalk Coffee Roasters, where I get the blue cheese croffle.
