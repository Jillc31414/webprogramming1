Ramen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of instant ramen (any flavor but preferably beef or shrimp)
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp Japanese BBQ sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • Furikake for topping

Instructions:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot.
  2. Add the instant ramen noodles and cook according to package instructions.
  3. Strain the noodles and put them back in the pot. Add 1 egg and allow it to start to cook then scramble into the noodles.
  4. Add the seasoning packet, Japanese BBQ sauce, brown sugar and sesame oil.
  5. Transfer the ramen to a bowl, top with furikake, and enjoy!
